Work at JavaJam

Barista

We are always looking for new, entusiastic baristas to join our growing JavaJam family! With extremely flexible hours, paid-time-off, and free concert experience, this job is perfect for anyone looking to hone their latte art while meeting exciting new people!

We are looking for people willing to do the following:

  • Make quality drinks for our ever-expanding customer base
  • Take customer orders and handle cash properly
  • Maintain a tidy workspace and dining room

Starting rate: $15/hr

Lighting Operator

We are searching for a trained lighting operator to help illuminate our many performances! Our stage is equipped with a state-of-the-art lighting panel, and we need someone with the skills to man it!

We will be looking to hire someone with the following skills:

  • Ability to properly use a professional lighting system
  • Talented in the art of lighting performances
  • Willing to work with hired talent in finding the best lighting to suit their sets

Starting rate: $22/hr
